Last updated: 2026-06-06

LumaLeaf helps indoor plant owners measure nearby light, keep plant care records, review possible plant health issues, and manage simple treatment checklists.

Contact: use the developer email listed on the LumaLeaf Google Play Store page.

Privacy Policy

LumaLeaf stores plant records, light measurements, diagnosis answers, and checklist status locally on your device. Advertising is provided by Google Mobile Ads. In-app purchases are handled by Google Play Billing.

Camera permission is requested only when you choose camera-based plant photo, identification, or diagnosis features. Notification permission is requested only when you turn on optional reminders.

LumaLeaf does not sell personal information. Plant health guidance is informational and does not replace professional cultivation, medical, or pest-control advice.

LumaLeaf는 개인정보를 판매하지 않습니다. 식물 상태 안내는 참고용이며 전문 재배, 의료, 방제 조언을 대체하지 않습니다.
